About me

Hi, I’m Paulo. I believe that equitable access to information and technology creates a more just and inclusive world. I believe in less but better, because it concentrates on the essential aspects, and the products/designs are not burdened with the non-essentials. To do that we have to go back to the purity and back to simplicity.

I have a Degree in Design and Interactive Media which was focused on the ability to forge a fundamental combination of key aspects to be successful in the creative process and also have a Masters in Multimedia which was focused on the ability to innovate in the design of new products, processes and services based on emerging technologies.

Aside from all this I live to travel (11 countries and counting), I like music, love photography, and I care deeply about equality.

I’m based in London, but originally portuguese from the tiny island of Portugal called Madeira Island. Thanks for stopping by.
